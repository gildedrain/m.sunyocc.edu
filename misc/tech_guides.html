Accessing the College Network

Access to the OCC network is strictly controlled by the use of a user name and password to protect your privacy and comply with federal law. Do not share this information with anyone.

Your user name and initial password is automatically created when you first register for a credit class at the college. Returning students keep their same user name and password as last semester.

User Name

  • Your user name is made up of the first initial of your first name followed by a period + your middle initial (if you provided one to us in the past) followed by a period + your full last name.
    • Example: John J. Smith = j.j.smith (lower case)
  • If your user name is not unique based on this rule, a random number will be added to the end of your last name (for example, j.j.smith398).

Don't know your user name?You can log on to an Internet Café computer and find out what your user name is by selecting What's my User ID? or by contacting the Helpdesk.

Passwords

In addition to your user name, you must enter a secret password when you log on. To help you remember your password, we recommend using the same password for your network, e-mail and WebAccess accounts.

  • First Time Password
    • The very first time you log on, your password will be your date of birth (format: mmddyy). The system will require you to change this password the first time you log on.
  • Creating a New Password
    • Your password must be 6-9 characters in length including letters and numbers. Your password must not include any part of your name. In WebAccess, be sure to write a hint to help you remember your password.
  • Passwords will expire and need to be changed every 365 days. You will be notified when they are ready to expire.
    • When your network password expires and you set up a new password, you can keep your WebAccess password the same by selecting the Change password option on the WebAccess Student Menu.

Don't remember your password?You can have your password reset by going to the Academic Computing Center (please have picture ID) or by calling the Helpdesk.

Logging on for the First Time

  1. Log on to the Clasnet domain from any computer.
  2. Change your password.
  3. Once this is done, you will be able to access your e-mail and the library databases from any computer either on or off campus.
  4. If you encounter a problem, please contact the Helpdesk.
